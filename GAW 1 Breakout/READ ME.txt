Joshua Simpson - Game a Week 1 - Modernise a Classic - Breakout Remake, BREAKOUT INFINATE

BREAKOUT INFINATE is an alternate take on the original Breakout for the Atari2600, featuring:
- Continiously spawning walls of bricks
- Power-up's - Extra Ball, Extra Life, Delay Wall Spawning

The objective is achieving the highest score possible.

Controls:
A & Left Arrow - Move left
D & Right Arrow - More Right
Space - Fire Ball from pad
Escape - Exit Game
